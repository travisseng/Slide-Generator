 # Erwin Raisz

---
<style scoped>p,li {font-size:0.84em}</style>

 # Early Life and Education

- Born on October 13, 1912, in Budapest, Hungary
- Family moved to Germany when he was 5 years old
- Graduated from the University of Berlin with a degree in geography and cartography
- Received his Ph.D. in geography from the University of Marburg

---
<style scoped>p,li {font-size:0.84em}</style>

 # Career
- Worked at the University of Wisconsin-Madison as a professor of geography and cartography
- Founded the Department of Geography and developed the graduate program in Geographic Information Science (GIS)
- Published numerous papers on GIS, spatial analysis, and map design
- Co-founded the Journal of Geographic Information System (JGIS) with Dr. Jack D. Johnson


---
<style scoped>p,li {font-size:0.88em}</style>

 # Later Life and Death
- Retired from the University of Wisconsin-Madison in 1986
- Moved to California and continued to work on GIS research and consulting
- Passed away on May 27, 2005, at the age of 92

Erwin Raisz was a pioneer in the field of Geographic Information Science and his contributions to the development of GIS technology have had a lasting impact on the field.
